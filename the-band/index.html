THE BAND

Rockin' Since 1994!

Smash Mouth is an American rock band from San Jose, California. The band was formed in 1994. They are best known for their songs "Walkin' on the Sun", "All Star", and a cover of The Monkees' "I'm a Believer". The band adopts retro styles covering decades of popular songs, including "Why Can't We Be Friends?", "Don't You (Forget About Me)", "Can't Get Enough of You Baby", "Getting Better", and "I Wan'na Be Like You".
