CS 1430 Reflection

Overall this was a pretty fun class. I had been web programming for some time now with PHP, Bootstrap, and Javascript, but this helped me refine my raw HTML and CSS skills. Learning how to make websites from scratch without the help of a framework was admittedly challenging, but it was worth it and rewarding.

I feel much more confident in my ability to take an idea or concept and turn it into a working model in code. I really enjoyed learning and being pushed to improve the design elements. In particular, I learned how to use full background images and how to make text and forms look aesthetically pleasing on top of them.

The confidence and eye for aesthetics and usability I've gained will be of assistance to me as I move forward in the development community and continue with my schooling.
